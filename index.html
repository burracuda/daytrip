Our Vision

An archive where the distance between artist and audience is zero. A world where every collaboration, single, and B-side is celebrated and discoverable, giving fans the power to explore the full creative journey.

This project is a comprehensive, interactive archive of the musical discography of the production duo Take A Daytrip. All data has been meticulously cross-referenced from official sources to create a definitive resource.
